## Our Pledge
We pledge to make participation in our project a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards
Be respectful, inclusive, and considerate in communication.

## Enforcement Responsibilities
Project maintainers are responsible for enforcing the code of conduct.

## Scope
This code applies within all project spaces and interactions.

## Enforcement
Instances of abusive behavior may be reported and will be reviewed.

## Enforcement Guidelines
Violations may result in warnings or removal from the project.
